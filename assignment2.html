    I found several common themes among the websites I reviewed. Streaming was a popular category,and was included in every website I reviews. Video games were also popular, and there was surprisingly specific video games that were very common, most notably Rocket League and Call of Duty. News was also a repeated category, though descriptions of this varied more. News media may be a sensitive category right now given the current political environment, and that may be contributing to a desire among students to either remain vague about their prefered news media or to be sure and portray a media diet they consider "balanced".
